Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a solar installation take? +

Most residential solar installations are completed within 1-3 days, depending on the system size and complexity. Commercial installations may take 1-2 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

What is the cost of a solar installation? +

Solar installation costs vary based on system size, location, and specific requirements. On average, residential systems range from $10,000 to $30,000. We offer free consultations and detailed quotes with no obligation. Financing options are also available.

Do you provide maintenance services? +

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance packages to keep your solar system operating at peak efficiency. Regular maintenance includes cleaning, inspections, and performance monitoring. We also provide 24/7 support for any issues.

What warranty do you offer? +

We provide comprehensive warranties covering equipment, installation workmanship, and system performance. Most solar panels come with 25-year warranties, and we offer extended warranty options for additional peace of mind.

Can solar panels work during power outages? +

Standard grid-tied solar systems shut down during power outages for safety reasons. However, we offer battery backup systems that can provide power during outages, keeping your essential appliances running.

Are there any government incentives available? +

Yes, there are various federal and state incentives, tax credits, and rebates available for solar installations. We'll help you navigate these programs and maximize your savings. Our team stays updated on all available incentives.

What are the benefits of a water softener system? +

Water softener systems eliminate hard water minerals, protecting your appliances from scale buildup, reducing soap usage, improving water taste and quality, and extending the life of your plumbing and water-using appliances. You'll also notice softer skin and hair, cleaner dishes, and no more hard water stains.

How long does water softener installation take? +

Most water softener installations are completed within 2-4 hours. We'll assess your water quality, determine the right system size for your home, and complete the installation professionally with minimal disruption to your daily routine.

Do you offer PURONICS water treatment systems? +

Yes, we are an authorized independent dealer of PURONICS Water Products. We offer EPA-registered, certified water treatment systems including whole-house water softeners and point-of-use solutions. All our systems meet the highest quality standards and come with professional installation and support.

How often does a water softener need maintenance? +

Water softeners require minimal maintenance. You'll need to add salt to the brine tank regularly (typically monthly, depending on usage). We provide maintenance guidelines and are available for periodic inspections to ensure your system continues operating efficiently. Most systems are designed to run automatically with minimal intervention.
